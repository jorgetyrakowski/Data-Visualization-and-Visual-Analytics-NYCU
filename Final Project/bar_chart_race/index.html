About this visualization

Dynamic Racing Bar Charts: Total and Per Capita CO₂ Emissions

This interactive visualization presents two different perspectives on CO₂ emissions through animated racing bar charts, allowing viewers to track changes in both total national emissions and emissions per person over time.

What do these charts show?

  • Displays real-time rankings of countries by CO₂ emissions
  • Toggles between total emissions and per capita views
  • Shows dynamic changes in country positions over time
  • Includes interactive controls for playback and reset

Key insights:

  • Reveals different leaders when comparing total vs per capita emissions
  • Highlights the impact of population size on emissions metrics
  • Shows historical shifts in global emission patterns
  • Demonstrates the rise of emerging economies in recent decades

Two Perspectives on Emissions:

Total Emissions View:
  • Shows absolute CO₂ output by country
  • Highlights largest overall contributors
  • Reflects industrial development and economic scale
  • Important for understanding global impact
Per Capita View:
  • Shows emissions per person in each country
  • Accounts for population differences
  • Reflects individual carbon footprints
  • Important for equity considerations
Source: Our World in Data (2024)
Note: Data includes CO₂ emissions from fossil fuels and industrial processes. Per capita calculations use annual population estimates.
