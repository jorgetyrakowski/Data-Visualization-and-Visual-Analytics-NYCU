Global CO2 Emissions Visualization

Dataset: CO2 Emissions by Our World in Data

geniASD

This visualization system provides an interactive exploration of global CO2 emissions data from 1800 to present, revealing historical patterns, emission sources, and the relationship between economic growth and carbon footprint across nations.

Available Visualizations:

  • 1. Bar Chart Race

    Dynamically visualizes the changes in CO2 emissions across countries over time, offering both total emissions and per capita views. Watch as countries rise and fall in their emission rankings through history.

  • 2. Choropleth Map

    Displays CO₂ Emissions in Relation to GDP, helping understand how economic development relates to carbon emissions across different regions of the world.

  • 3. Stacked Area Chart

    Shows the evolution of global CO₂ emissions by source over time, breaking down contributions from various sectors including coal, oil, gas, cement production, and other industrial processes.

Select any visualization from the navigation bar above to begin exploring the data.
